Heritable genome editing in C. elegans via a crisPr-cas9 system

CRISPR Cas9 and single guiderna was used to disrupt specific segment of the C.ele dna and caused mutation. A step by step procedure was provided on how the cas9 was configured. This paper explain how to determine the gene disruption both in case where there is obvious phenotype or associated also how to find when there is no obvious phenotype linked.
